    The GST rates on the services of packers and movers are 18% for the complete shifting service (packing, loading, transportation, unloading, and unpacking). However, the GST rate is only 5% if you use only the transport service of movers and packers.

  • How much will the moving company charge as an insurance fee?

    The professional moving company will charge 3% of the total value of the declared items as an insurance fee. It is not mandatory to obtain insurance on your move. If you don’t want it, you can say no to insurance before hiring the service.

  • What are the cost-heads in a moving price estimate?

    A moving estimate will comprise many cost heads. The main cost heads are as follows:

    • Loading and unloading charge
    • Transportation cost
    • Unpacking charge
    • Insurance fee (if availed)
    • GST charge
    • Toll tax
    • Octroi

  • On what basis do packers and movers determine the costs of moving services?

    In general, professional packers and movers determine the moving cost on the following basis.

    • Packing materials required for packaging

    • Insurance if you need it
    • Applicable taxes such as GST, toll tax, and Octroi

  • What is the best time to schedule a move?

    The season or timing of the relocation is one of the many factors that influence the costs of moving from one place to another. Therefore, we recommend choosing the right date and time for moving.

    In terms of affordability, the best time to move from one place to another is during weekdays or in the middle of the month. Weekends or holidays are expensive dates to move due to the high demand for moving services. So, it is better to avoid weekends or holidays to move if it is possible.
