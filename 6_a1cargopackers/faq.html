Frequently Asked Questions

General

How do I book?

Use the quote form, call us at 8121168811, or email a1logisticspackers@gmail.com.

Can I reschedule/cancel?

Yes, inform us 24 hours in advance. Cancellation fees may apply close to the move.

Pricing

What affects the cost?

Volume/weight, distance, floor access, packing type, and special items (piano, safe, etc.).

Are there hidden charges?

No. We share an itemized estimate with all applicable taxes.

Services

Do you dismantle and reinstall furniture?

Yes, for most standard beds, wardrobes, and modular furniture.

How long does a move take?

Local: same day; Domestic: depends on distance & route. We share ETA with tracking.
