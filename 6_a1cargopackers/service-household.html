Overview

Moving homes is a big milestone. Our trained crew uses export‑grade packing materials, protective floor coverings, and smart labeling to ensure every item reaches safely. From 1RK to villa — we tailor the plan to your needs.

  • Door‑to‑door service

Step‑by‑Step Process

  1. Pre‑Move Survey: Virtual/onsite assessment, customized estimate.
  2. Packing: Multi‑layer protection, wardrobe cartons, TV/AC secure packs.
  3. Loading: Trolleys, sliders, ramps; inventory matched to vehicle.
  4. Transport: GPS tracking, route planning, live updates.
  5. Delivery & Unpacking: Placement by rooms + debris removal.

FAQs

How do you protect fragile items?

We use bubble wrap, foam corners, double‑wall cartons, and label them for top‑load only.

Do you offer insurance?

Yes, full‑value and comprehensive transit insurance with quick documentation.

Can I choose the move date?

Absolutely. We work with your schedule, including weekends and holidays.
