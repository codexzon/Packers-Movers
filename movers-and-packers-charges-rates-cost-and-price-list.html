Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the cost of local moving?

    Usually, the cost of moving ranges from Rs. 4,000 to Rs. 20,000 for local house shifting with a national average of Rs. 7,000.

  • What is the cost of intercity or interstate moving?

    The cost of intercity or interstate moving depends on a number of factors including the distance travelled in transit, manpower required, how much stuff you have, and how far you are moving. On the lowest range of intercity relocation cost, you can expect Rs. 11,000 for 1 BHK; the high end can reach up to Rs. 60,000 for 4+ BHK.

  • How much does it cost to move locally from one apartment to another?

    In the case of local moving, the cost of moving from one apartment to another will be as follows.

    • 1 BHK apartment shifting charge: Rs. 4,000 - 11,000
    • 2 BHK apartment shifting charge: Rs. 6,500 - 16,000
    • 3 BHK apartment shifting charge: Rs. 10,000 - 20,000
  • What are the costs of car transportation from one city to another?

    The costs of intercity home relocation will chiefly depend on the type of vehicle and the distance travelled in transit. On the lowest spectrum of car transportation charge, you can expect Rs. 5,500 - 11,000 for 100 – 350 km, the upper range can be Rs. 15,000 - 26,000 for 2300 – 3000 km. Other price ranges are as follows.

    • Car transportation charges for 350 to 750 km distance: Rs. 7,500 - 14,000
    • Car transportation charges for 750 – 1200 km distance: Rs. 9,000 - 17,000
    • Car transportation charges for 1200 – 1700 km distance: Rs. 11,000 – 20,000
    • Car transportation charges for 1700 – 2300 km distance: Rs. 13,000 - 23,000
  • How much do movers charge to transport a bike from one city to another?

    The cost of transporting a bike from one city to another will depend on how far your destination is. The lowest price range for bike transportation service is Rs. 2,800 - 5,800 for a 100 – 350 km distance. The upper price range for bike transport service is Rs. 8,000 - 15,000 for a 2300 – 3000 km distance.

  • What are the charges for a villa or bungalow shifting?

    A bungalow or villa usually contains more items than 1 to 3 BHK apartments. Therefore, movers and packers will charge you more for the relocation of the bungalow or villa than for the relocation of the apartment. For local shifting, they will charge around Rs. 18,000 – 28,000. We recommend allowing movers to evaluate your belongings for a villa or bungalow shifting, especially if you are moving from one city to another.

  • What is the cost of office shifting within a city?

    The cost of local office shifting will range from Rs 11,000 to Rs 1,70,000. The price will vary depending on the size of your office - micro-office, small office, medium office, standard office, and corporate office.   • What is the cost of office/corporate relocation from one city to another?

    The price of an office or corporate relocation from one city to another will depend on many factors, including your current city, the destination city, and the size of your office. The price range for intercity office relocation services will be around Rs 11,000 – 60,000 for small offices and Rs 24,000 – 2,00,000 for medium to standard offices. If you are looking at charges for large corporation office relocation, then it is better to contact us for quotations.

  • What are the charges for moving insurance in India?

    There are two types of moving insurance offered by packers and movers - goods transit insurance at the rate of 1.5% and all-inclusive insurance at the rate of 3%.

  • What are the GST rates for packers and movers in India?

    The GST rate for hiring packers and movers is 5 % only if you use only a transport (logistic) service. If you will use full service of packers and movers then GST rates will be at the rate of 18%.

  • What are the standard charges for warehousing & storage of household items?

    If you get warehousing & storage service from packers and movers then they will charge you around Rs. 3,000 per month for 1 BHK household items and Rs. 4,000 per month for 2 BHK household iteHL

  • What are the different cost heads included in a moving estimate?

    Different cost heads included in a typical moving estimate include:

    • Packing charge (includes the cost of packing materials and boxes)
    • Labour charge (includes loading and unloading charges)
    • Transportation cost (includes the fuel consumption in transit)
    • Insurance charge (optional)
    • Warehousing and storage charge, if required
    • Unpacking charge (make it clear whether it is included or not)
  • Are GST, toll tax, and octroi included in a moving estimate?

    No. Usually, GST, toll tax, and octroi are not included in a moving estimate. These charges will be extra.
